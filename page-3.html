Brittain Hadley

Brittain Hadley - Chartered Surveyors, I worked on revamping their online presence. I redesigned their previously blocky, text-heavy, and inconsistent website, using my skills to create a cleaner, more streamlined layout and introduced colours, striking imagery and immersive elements that offered an engaging and approachable user experience. Additionally, I developed a social media calendar for the project administrator to enhance organisation and efficiency, significantly streamlining their online appeal.

Utopia

Utopia was inspired by Tyler Mitchell's vision of Black utopia, celebrating cultural identities through vibrant surroundings. It was a particularly meaningful project for me as I collaborated with close friends to embody freedom, joy, and peace through each image.

Working as both photographer and art director, I carefully used props, colours, locations, and body language(poses) to convey the warmth and essence of utopia.

Tree of Codes

In collaboration w/ Jagjot Golhar & Studio Wayne McGregor

For this project, I explored the interplay between technology and physical movement by reimagining studio Wayne McGregor's Tree of Codes — a choreographic performance. My contributions focused on post-production, including crafting the narrative, shaping the clip's arc, editing, and presentation. The piece was projected onto a side wall, illuminating the dimly lit hallway with visually captivating imagery that immersed viewers in a journey blending reality and mirrored reflections.
